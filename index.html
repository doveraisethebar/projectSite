Hey you!

While we complain about the traffic, the demands of work at uni , the angry boss who did not like what we presented for the trimester project, we end up forgetting some simple good things that happened, at least once, during our day. We live our busy lives, we forget that there are people suffering with eating disorders, breast cancer, lack of sanitation, domestic violence and gender inequality and forget that with small doings we can bring significant impact!

How?

So here's a challenge : for the next 60 days!

Once a day, you’ll share your beauty moment, and this will be transformed into happy vibes for the cause you think is most important!

How would that work?

Every picture that you post has to be directed to one social cause. Each cause will be represented by a type of Dove Beauty Bar.

The ratings for each category will be change according to the number of pictures posted towards each cause. The one with the highest number of hits, will receive our helping hand!
